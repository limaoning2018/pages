The influence of children's educational game achievement system on learning motivation：Double mediating effect of GUES and Enjoyment

 

Abstract

At present,the value and role of children's digital game achievement system for game education have not attracted public’s attention. Due to lack of related experience and theories,the design and research of game achievement system lack theoretical basis and reference experience.In this paper,we study the influence of the achievement system of the children's educational games on learning motivation, and the mediating effect between game user experience satisfaction(GUES)and enjoyment.We used structural equation model to find out the relationship and role. The results show that the achievement system has a significant impact on learning motivation through the mediating effect of GUES and enjoyment.GUES and enjoument are important predictors.The five components of achievement system are all related to the use of GUES and e enjoyment.Reward is an important factor affecting GUES,  with social interaction is an important factor affecting enjoyment. Enhancing Social and Reward can help improve the influence of achievement system on learning motivation.

Key words: Children's educational games,Game achievement system, Learning motivation,GUES, enjoyment

 

1.Introduction

1.1. Background of the research

In the future of 2023, players of Digital Games will soon reach the number of 3 billion[1].91% of Children aged from 2 to 17 are the main players of Digital games[2] and educational digital games have become a main part in children’s lives and entertainment<table 1>.Piaget's theory of cognitive development stage holds that abstract ability and concentration ability and self-control ability are limited in the middle stage of childhood (6-12 years old).Among them,the main problem in designing children’s DEG is how to use of the game achievement system to improve the learning motivation, increase the participation, improve the learning results, and achieve the best balance between entertainment and learning.

1.2.The researching problem

The current Children’s DEG achievement system is a simple copy of the successful commercial entertainment game achievement system and it ignores  ignores educational functionality and user particularity.It brings negative experience and reduces the quality of learning.As the core component of DEG,the lack of positive role on learning motivation and the lack of action rules rules, mechanism, and leading factors brings great trouble to game designers, which leads to chaos and failure of the design of achievement system.Some of the research is limited in studying the badge or a specific achievement type as the achievement system and it ignore the multidimensional and richness of the components of the achievement system. And it ignores the impact of the comprehensive elements on the learning motivation, also misleads the designers ' understanding of the achievement system.

1.3. The purpose and necessity of the research

The purpose of the research:①To empirically examine the influence of children's DEG achievement system on learning motivation and the double mediating effect of game user experience satisfaction(GUES) and enjoyment.②To verify the correlation between the elements of achievement system and GUES and enjoyment.③Find out the main factors of the achievement system that affect GUES, enjoyment and learning motivation.Through the research on how the achievement system can affect the learning motivation,the function,the relationship and mechanism,this paper provides valuable reference and necessary support for the research and design of children's DEG achievement system.

1.4. The methods of the research

Through some previous research, analysis and expert review,we built conceptual models and made assumptions.We took children as the research objects and conducted the survey.After the data were collected, descriptive analysis, reliability and validity analysis and correlation analysis were carried out by SPSS22.0.Amos was used for confirmatory factor analysis.Structural equation model was used to conduct empirical research and systematic verification of the hypothesis. The results were analyzed and the final conclusion was drawn < Table 2 >.

 

2. Literature Review

2.1. Children's Digital Educational Games.

Digital Educational Games are the games[3] that are used for educational purpose.They includes both learning and gaming mechanism so they can provide entertainment and educational positive experience[4] [5].They are considered the most potential tool for effective learning[6] [7] [8] [9].A large number of studies have shown that DEG enhance users' learning motivation, bring changes in learning attitude and behavior, and improve learning efficiency and academic performance.Children's DEG is a learning tool specially developed for child users.It combines entertainment with learning and becomes an activity that children are interested in. It is regarded as the best way to stimulate children's learning motivation.

2.2. Game achievement system

Game achievement system is a set of tasks that are set by game designers. Players can get a number of rewards when they complete the tasks or reach some kind of status and it is regarded as an important part of the game platform[10].The achievements can be shown,managed and shared across games[11].The achievement system enriches the game playing methods and tasks so it is an economic and effective way to prolong the life of the game[12].Players can use different gameplay and difficulties to complete different achievements and get different rewards. This method produces different experience for gamers and reduces development costs. Players can get satisfaction and confidence when they are sharing or showing off the achievements. This process can also lengthening the playing time and the reputation of the game. Achievement system is closely related to the player's action performance, enthusiasm and attitude.

2.3. Learning motivation

Learning motivation is the intrinsic belief[13] that triggers individual learning goals, induces learning behavior, strengthens cognitive processes, and improves learning outcomes. Also it is the direct reason and inner strength that push the learners to learn. The ARCS model proposed by J.M. Keller in 1983(A-attention,R-relationship,C-confidence,S-satisfaction)expounds the main influencing factors of learning motivation, arguing that the use of these four elements can motivate and maintain students’ learning motivation and propose systematic methods to identify and solve the problem about learning motivation[14]. The strategies proposed under the ARCS model can effectively improve learning motivation. Now it is widely used to assess student learning motivation[15,16].

2.4. Game User Experience Satisfaction

GUES(Game User Experience Satisfaction) is the extent that the how the player is satisfied with his own experience while playing digital games.It explains the quality of the player's interaction with the game.GUES is a multi - dimensional structure,which involves immersion, interest, pleasure, aesthetics, motivation and many other contents.It is an important factor to measure the value and effectiveness of educational games[17].The improvement of GUES will have a positive impact on learning motivation and learning results.In 2016, Phan proposed the Game User Experience Satisfaction Scale (GUESS) to measure and evaluate users’ satisfaction[17].

2.5. Game Enjoyment

Game enjoyment is a kind of status or action that players can get enjoyment, comfort and satisfaction form digital games. Enjoyment is a multi - dimensional concept,which includes physiological, cognitive and emotional comprehensive experience. Making players get enjoyment is the most important goal for digital games. Enjoyment is also an important standard for players to measure the game[19]. In effective DEG, enjoying pleasure can stimulate the player 's learning initiative, affect the player' s learning motivation and learning results. The EGameFlow scale for evaluating the digital education game enjoyment is an industry recognized effective tool for its effectiveness and reliability[20].

3.3 Participants

The sample data came from 475 children in 6 Chinese cities.22 invalid questionnaires were deleted and 453 valid samples were recovered. The effective recovery rate was 95.3%,and the average age of the participants is 8.7 years old. Among them,236 boys and 217 girls were included.

3.4. Research and Procedures

The components of the achievement system and and problems of the measurement have been studied and verified and they had good reliability and validity. We used ARCS learning motivation scale to measure the learning motivation. The GUESS-18 scale was used to measure GUES. And the EGameFlow scale was used to measure enjoyment. We simplified and optimized the questionnaire into questions. Expert review was used to determined the questionnaire. Investigators conducted an access questionnaire survey on the participants and the data effectiveness was analyzed through SPSS. We used AMOS software to analyze he sample data through the reliability and validity detection.Then,we systematically verified the hypothesis through the empirical study of the structural equation model.

5. Research Conclusion

After combining with literature analysis and previous research,we constructed the model of children's educational game achievement system and learning motivation.And we verified the hypothesis of the positive influence of achievement system on learning motivation under the dual mediation effect of GUES and Enjoyment. The conclusions are as follows:

     (1) GUES directly affects users 'cognition, attitude, behavior and effectiveness and it is an important predictor of children' s learning quality in DEG.The elements of DEG achievement system have a positive impact on GUES.The common effect brings a significant improvement in GUES, indicating that the previous scholars only isolated incentive impact on learning motivation as it is not comprehensive.In path analysis,we found that "reward" needs to be paid attention in the design of achievement system as the key factor for the biggest impact on GUES. While improving the overall quality of achievement system, strengthening "reward" will better promote GUES.

(2) In previous research, the relationship between DEG achievement system and Game enjoyment has not attracted attention.Research has found that the achievement system components have a positive impact on Game enjoyment, and "social" is the key factor for the biggest impact on enjoyment as social ability brings more Game enjoyment to the players through sharing,challenging and competing,which adds players’ interest and immersion in the game.Enhancing social ability in the design of achievement systems can bring a stronger enjoyment experience to the game.

(3)GUES and Enjoyment have a positive role on learning motivation.We also explain the

complex reasons affecting the changes in learning motivation when illustrate the principles of intermediary variables.We proved the opinion that single factor can affect the change of learning motivation is wrong,which filled the gap in previous study.The design of the DEG achievement system should focus on GUES、Enjoyment and adding novel and attractive elements to enable players to feel immersed,satisfied and curious when sharing,challenging and competing,and thus promote the enhancement of players ' learning motivation.

  (4) DEG achievement system has significant influence on learning motivation. The verification of GUES and Enjoyment reveals the reasons and rules of the achievement system on learning motivation.The structural equation model can serve as an important tool to measure the effect of the achievement system on learning motivation and it has great value in the design and development of achievement system.This study corrects and discusses the errors in previous research and draws our attention to the problem.It attracts more researchers to carry out research to study the educational game achievement system.

6. Future research and limitations

In future researches,we have deepe study the consistant impact of DEG achievement system on learning and explain its value.The role of GUES and enjoyment,the differences and rules under the conditions of difficulty, frequency and intensity of achievement system, and the differences and changes under the conditions of different ages, genders and preferences will be studied in the future.

The limitations are as follows: First,due to the limitation of the number of words in the paper, the research needs to be in-depth.Players of different ages, genders and preferences may show different attitudes towards the achievement system of educational games.And we will have deeper study in the future.Secondly, since the children who participated in the study are too young to have the ability to complete the questionnaire independently,we will finish the questionnaire by interviewing,and the answers may be affected by the visitors.The reliability and validity of the data can be effectively improved by increasing the number of samples.Finally, the difference of game experience level may affect the data.Limited by the conditions,deeper research have not been done, but the follow-up research will gradually solve the problem.
